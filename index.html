Features

🎯

Precise Loop Points

Set a start and end time on any track with fine-grained control. Isolate a four-bar phrase, a tricky chord change, or a single dance eight-count.

♾️

Infinite Looping

Your chosen section plays on repeat until you're ready to move on. No interruptions, no manual restarting — just focused repetition.

🎵

Apple Music & Spotify

Works with the music you already have. InfiLooper integrates natively with both Apple Music and Spotify on your Mac.

☰

Lives in the Menu Bar

Always accessible without cluttering your workspace. InfiLooper sits quietly in your macOS menu bar, ready whenever you need it.

🖼️

Album Artwork

See the album art of what's playing at a glance, so you always know exactly what track you're looping.

🔒

Private by Design

InfiLooper collects zero personal data. It only fetches album artwork from the internet — nothing else leaves your Mac.

How it works

01

Play your track

Start playing any song in Apple Music or Spotify as you normally would.

02

Open InfiLooper

Click the InfiLooper icon in your menu bar to bring up the control panel.

03

Set your loop

Mark the start and end points of the passage you want to repeat.

04

Practice away

InfiLooper takes care of the rest — looping your selection on repeat.
