Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026  ·  Last updated: January 1, 2026

The short version: InfiLooper does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app runs entirely on your Mac. The only network activity is fetching album artwork from Apple Music or Spotify — no personal information is involved in that request.

1. Who we are

InfiLooper is a macOS application developed and distributed independently on the Mac App Store. This Privacy Policy explains how InfiLooper handles information in connection with your use of the app.

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at support@infilooper.app.

2. Information we do not collect

InfiLooper does not collect, transmit, or store any of the following:

  • Personal identifiers such as your name, email address, or Apple ID
  • Device identifiers or advertising identifiers
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or telemetry
  • Your listening history or music library data
  • Location data of any kind
  • Any other personally identifiable information

No data is sent to any server operated by InfiLooper or any third party.

3. Network activity — album artwork only

The only outbound network request InfiLooper makes is to fetch album artwork for the currently playing track. This request is made directly to Apple's or Spotify's content delivery network using a standard image URL provided by the respective service.

This image fetch:

  • Does not include any personal information
  • Does not identify you to Apple, Spotify, or anyone else
  • Is the same type of request your browser makes when loading any image on the web

4. Permissions

InfiLooper requests the following macOS permissions:

  • Apple Music access: To read the currently playing track's title, artist, and position so it can control looping. This data never leaves your Mac.
  • Spotify access: To read the currently playing track information from Spotify. This data never leaves your Mac.

You will be shown a macOS permission prompt the first time InfiLooper needs access to each service. You can revoke these permissions at any time in System Settings → Privacy & Security.

5. Third-party services

InfiLooper does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app does not contain any tracking SDKs.

When you use InfiLooper alongside Spotify or Apple Music, those apps and services operate under their own privacy policies, which are separate from this one:

  • Apple Privacy Policy
  • Spotify Privacy Policy

6. Data retention

Because InfiLooper does not collect any data, there is nothing to retain or delete. All app preferences (such as loop points) are stored locally on your Mac using standard macOS storage mechanisms and never leave your device.

7. Children's privacy

InfiLooper does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app contains no data collection of any kind.

8. Changes to this policy

If this Privacy Policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Since InfiLooper collects no personal data, any changes are unlikely to affect your privacy in a meaningful way.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or about InfiLooper, please reach out at support@infilooper.app.
